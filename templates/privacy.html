Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

True Belonging LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Inclusive JD Optimizer tool at inclusivejd.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. What We Collect

When you use the Inclusive JD Optimizer, we process the job description text you submit. We do not store your job description text after processing. We collect only the minimum data necessary to process your payment ($1 unlock fee) via Stripe.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Job description text is sent to Google's Gemini API for AI analysis and is not stored by us.
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card details.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Google Gemini API

Job descriptions you submit are processed by Google's Gemini AI API. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API data.

4. GDPR / CCPA Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold. Since we do not store job description content, there is typically no personal data to retrieve. For payment records, contact Stripe directly. For any other requests, contact us at: trucolorsai@gmail.com

5. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We follow industry-standard security practices. Your job description content is never logged to disk or retained after your session.
